I am a final-year PhD candidate in space physics at [Rice University](https://physics.rice.edu/), advised by [Frank Toffoletto](https://profiles.rice.edu/faculty/frank-r-toffoletto). I am concurrently completing an MS in Computer Science (Data &amp; AI) at Rice, and earned my BS at the [Southern University of Science and Technology](https://www.sustech.edu.cn/) (SUSTech). My research studies the dynamics and transport of charged particles throughout Earth's magnetosphere, using global magnetohydrodynamic simulation and kinetic test-particle modeling. I also work on operational space-weather forecasting and on applying machine learning to space-physics questions.

I am also broadly interested in machine learning and AI as research subjects in their own right. My current interests cover many tasks, including LLM validation on long-horizon technical work and using LLMs to do full processor (CPU) design with ASIC physical implementation.

I am on the academic / industry job market this cycle. Please feel free to reach out at <a href="mailto:yz167@rice.edu">yz167@rice.edu</a>.
