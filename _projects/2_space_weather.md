Space weather &mdash; the chain of energetic phenomena driven by the Sun and felt at Earth &mdash; affects satellites, communications, GPS, and the power grid. Operational nowcasting connects scientific models of the magnetosphere to real-time data streams so warnings can be issued before particles or fields impact infrastructure.

Within this area I work on the following projects:

- **Operational RCM with NASA.** A streaming pipeline that drives the Rice Convection Model with real-time solar-wind input and flags candidate substorm injections at geosynchronous orbit.
- **Substorm injection physics.** Connecting empirical nowcasting predictors back to the underlying physics of plasma-sheet flow channels and dipolarization fronts.
