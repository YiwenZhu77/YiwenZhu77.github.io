Space weather, the chain of energetic phenomena driven by the Sun and felt at Earth, affects satellites, communications, GPS, and the power grid. Operational nowcasting connects scientific models of the magnetosphere to real-time data streams so that warnings can be issued before particles or fields impact infrastructure.

Within this area I work on the following projects:

- **Operational RCM with NASA.** A streaming pipeline that drives the Rice Convection Model with real-time solar-wind input and flags candidate substorm injections of energetic particles at geosynchronous orbit.
